# Dialed Self Growth Loops

## Purpose

Dialed Self grows when verified proof creates status, status creates sharing, sharing creates friend pressure, and friend pressure creates more proof.

Growth should not depend on generic reminders or spam. The product should make users want to show proof because the artifact looks good and the competition feels alive.

## Loop 1: Share After Proof

Trigger:

- User submits a meaningful proof.

Reward:

- Points/pending score.
- Pillar activated.
- Trust/confidence label.
- Share card CTA.

Share artifact:

- Entry Proof Card.
- Food Macro Card.
- Health Proof Card.

Required card elements:

- +points or "Scoring pending".
- Proof type.
- Pillar.
- Username.
- Dialed branding.
- "Proof > promises"
- "Get Dialed"
- Referral/deep-link placeholder.

Success metric:

- Proof submitted to share card opened.
- Share card opened to exported/shared.
- Shared card to referral visit/signup.

Product rule:

- Share cards should feel like status symbols, not receipts.

## Loop 2: Fully Dialed Day

Trigger:

- User completes Movement, Fuel, Mind, and Recovery in one day.

Reward:

- Fully Dialed Day banner.
- Full-screen celebration later.
- Gold/share card.
- Bonus Proof tomorrow.
- Streak boost/badge.

Share artifact:

- Fully Dialed Day Card.

Success metric:

- Fully Dialed Day frequency.
- Fully Dialed card exports.
- Next-day return rate after Fully Dialed Day.

Product rule:

- Fully Dialed Day should feel rare and celebratory.

## Loop 3: Friend Pressure

Trigger:

- Friend logs proof.
- Friend passes user.
- User is close to first place.
- Friend completes Fully Dialed Day.

Notification examples:

- "Kyle logged water at 6:04 AM. Hydration demon behavior."
- "Emma completed all 4 pillars."
- "You're 9 points from first."
- "Mason passed you by 12."
- "One Recovery Proof saves your streak."

Success metric:

- Notification open rate.
- Notification to proof submission.
- Leaderboard view after notification.

Product rule:

- No generic reminders. Every notification should reference proof, rank, streak, or friend context.

## Loop 4: Leaderboard FOMO

Trigger:

- User opens leaderboard.
- User sees close race.
- User sees pillar-specific weakness.

Modes:

- Today.
- This Week.
- All Time.
- Movement.
- Fuel.
- Mind.
- Recovery.
- Verified Only.
- Friends.
- Squads.
- Challenges.

CTA examples:

- "One proof can move you up."
- "You are 11 points behind Kyle."
- "One Food Proof puts you top 3."
- "Recovery is untouched. Fix it."

Success metric:

- Leaderboard view to proof submission.
- Leaderboard view to friend invite.
- Rank-card shares.

Product rule:

- Leaderboard scoring must favor verified proofs.

## Loop 5: Friend Comparison

Trigger:

- User views friend profile or leaderboard row.

Comparison example:

- David vs Kyle
- Movement: David
- Fuel: Kyle
- Mind: David
- Recovery: nobody
- Winner: David by 8

Share artifact:

- Friend Comparison Card.

Success metric:

- Comparison views.
- Comparison shares.
- Challenge friend CTA clicks.

Product rule:

- Comparison should be punchy, funny, and proof-based.

## Loop 6: Challenge Momentum

Trigger:

- User joins challenge.
- User logs challenge-eligible proof.
- User moves up challenge leaderboard.
- User wins badge/reward.

Challenge examples:

- 7-Day Fully Dialed.
- Hydration Week.
- Protein Streak.
- 10k Steps Club.
- Recovery Arc.
- 5AM Movement.
- Mind Monk.
- Gym Crew Battle.

Share artifacts:

- Challenge Join Card.
- Challenge Rank Card.
- Challenge Victory Card.

Success metric:

- Challenge joins.
- Challenge proof submissions.
- Challenge completion.
- Challenge card shares.
- Invites per challenge.

Product rule:

- Challenges must define proof requirements clearly.

## Loop 7: Proof Limit Upsell

Trigger:

- User has low or zero Daily Proofs.

Free earn paths:

- +1 by sharing today's score, max once/day.
- +1 tomorrow for Fully Dialed Day.
- +1 tomorrow for 3-day streak.
- +1 for invited friend signup.

Pro path:

- More Daily Proofs.
- More Food/Photo capacity.
- Auto Health scoring.
- Deeper analysis.

Copy:

- "You're out of Proofs."
- "Earn +1 by sharing today."
- "Come back tomorrow."
- "Pro unlocks more ways to prove the day."

Success metric:

- Out-of-proofs to share.
- Out-of-proofs to invite.
- Out-of-proofs to paywall view.
- Paywall to Pro conversion.

Product rule:

- Free users should still be able to play and improve.

## Loop 8: Daily Recap

Trigger:

- User has enough proof data for the day.

Reward:

- Witty recap.
- Strongest pillar.
- Missing pillar.
- Best proof.
- Tomorrow recommendation.
- Share quote.

Share artifact:

- Daily Recap Quote Card.

Success metric:

- Daily Recap opens.
- Recap shares.
- Recap recommendation to next-day proof.

Product rule:

- Daily Recap should feel like a reward, not a report card.

## Growth Copy Rules

Use:

- Proof > promises.
- Get Dialed.
- Fully Dialed Day.
- Verified Proof.
- Dialed Score.
- Daily Proofs left.
- One proof can move you up.

Avoid:

- Generic reminder copy.
- Clinical health language.
- Shame/body judgment.
- Chatbot framing.
- GPT branding.
- Manual check-in as primary scoring path.
